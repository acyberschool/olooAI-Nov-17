## 2. DTW (Delegate to Walter)
You will see **"DTW"** buttons across the platform (e.g., "Delegate Day Plan", "Generate Strategy").

**What is it?**
Instead of just recording data, DTW buttons trigger Walter to **think and act**.
*   **Example:** Clicking "Generate Themes" in the **Events** tab doesn't just open a form. Walter scans industry trends and instantly creates 3 strategic event themes for you.

## 4. Social Media Engine (Generative AI)
Navigate to the **Social Media** tab (or inside a Business Line).

*   **Calendar Generation:** Click "Create Calendar" -> "Chat with Walter". Tell him your goal (e.g., "Promote our summer sale"). He will fill your schedule.
*   **Visuals:** Click on any scheduled post day.
    *   **Image:** Click **"Nano Banana"** to generate an image based on the post content.
    *   **Video:** Click **"Veo 3"** to generate a short video asset (720p).

---

## 5. Collaboration (ATC)
Assign work to your team using natural language.

**How to use:**
In the voice recorder or text input, simply say:
> *"Create a task to update the slide deck and assign to @John."*

**Walter's Logic:**
1.  Walter detects **"@John"**.
2.  He searches your **Members & Access** list.
3.  He creates the task and tags John as the owner instantly.

---

## 6. Managing Your Team
Navigate to **Access** in the sidebar.
*   **Invite:** Type an email to send a Magic Link invite.
*   **Activity:** See exactly when your team members were last active.
*   **Permissions:** Toggle specific module access for each member.
